About the Project

The SSE Faculty Research Interview Program highlights the research, scholarship, innovation, and expertise of faculty within the University of Missouri–Kansas City School of Science and Engineering. Through recorded faculty interviews, research summaries, and expertise profiles, the program provides an accessible introduction to the diverse research activities taking place across the School.

Program Overview

Faculty interviews are conducted in a conversational format and focus on research interests, current projects, facilities, collaborations, funding activities, and opportunities for student involvement. Interview recordings are accompanied by AI-generated summaries to help visitors quickly identify areas of expertise and potential collaboration opportunities.

Program Activity

Faculty interviews are conducted on an ongoing basis and interview recordings, expertise profiles, and AI-generated summaries are published through this website as they become available.
