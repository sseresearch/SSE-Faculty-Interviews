Program Purpose

The SSE Faculty Research Interview Program was established to showcase faculty expertise, research activities, innovation, and collaboration opportunities across the School of Science and Engineering.

Objectives

  • Highlight faculty research and scholarship
  • Increase visibility of faculty expertise
  • Promote interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Support student engagement in research
  • Provide accessible research summaries and interviews

Audience

The website serves faculty, students, staff, prospective students, researchers, industry partners, and members of the public interested in learning more about research within SSE.
